What PTSD IS

PTSD or posttraumatic stress disorder is a mental disorder that is caused by traumatic events in one’s life.

It’s symptoms can cause involuntary memories, nightmares, flashbacks, emotional distress, and physiological reactions when exposed to triggers.

How We Help

We offer a number of services to help people with PTSD.

  • We offer a quiet space with T.V., video games, books,music, food
  • an overnight stay with nurses that take great care
  • service animals for outside treatment if required
